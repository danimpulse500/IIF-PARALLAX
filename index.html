About the Annual Convening on Impact Investing

The Annual Convening on Impact Investing (ACII) is Nigeria’s premier forum for networking, thought leadership, and capital mobilization. The platform bridges the gap between global investment and local potential, uniting policymakers, investors, and social entrepreneurs to catalyze sustainable solutions to unlock capital. Over two high-impact days, participants will engage in expert-led dialogues, unlock exclusive deal opportunities, and connect with the pioneers defining the continent's impact landscape.

Join us to drive the future of capital, culminating in our annual celebration of excellence at the Awards Ceremony.

About Impact Investors Foundation

Formerly known as the Impact Investing Alliance of Nigeria (IIAN), IIF was founded in 2019 as a multi-sector collaborative comprising the Ford Foundation, Africa Capital Alliance, BusinessDay Media, Bank of Industry and Dalberg Advisors to unlock private capital for social investments in Nigeria.
