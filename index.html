About the Annual Convening on Impact Investing

The Annual Convening on Impact Investing is the leading forum driving networking and impact investing thought leadership in Nigeria. The event constantly extends the frontiers of impact investing by bringing together the widest range of stakeholders to stimulate conversations aimed at birthing local solutions.

Hosted by the Impact Investors Foundation in collaboration with a group of impact thought leaders and institutions, the convening provides unique insights on issues from national and regional perspectives whilst drawing on the diverse global impact investing community excited about the opportunities Africa presents. The 2-day event will feature panel discussions; curated Deal Room sessions to connect businesses with impact funds and; facilitated Impact Fund Showcases which will introduce vibrant impact funds and ecosystem-building initiatives to investors and other catalytic capital providers.

The event culminates with the Annual Dinner and Awards Ceremony to recognise impactful businesses and investors as well as philanthropic, non-profit and civil society organisations under the Innocent Chukwuma Award for Social Impact.

About IIF

The Impact Investors Foundation (IIF) was established in 2018 as a collaborative effort among several prominent organisations, including the Ford Foundation, Africa Capital Alliance, BusinessDay Media, Bank of Industry, and Dalberg Advisors.

Its primary mission is to unlock capital for social investments in Nigeria, serving as the secretariat for the National Advisory Board for Impact Investing (NABII).
